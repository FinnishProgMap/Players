About the Map

This map traces the presence and connections between core players in the first wave of the Finnish prog rock scene and adjacent progressive, avant-garde and experimental music scenes from the late 60s–70s. It was developed as part of an ongoing research project on the distinguishing characteristics of Finnish progressive rock and the links between individual artistic identity, cultural identity and sound; in particular, to understand why and how the Finnish scene developed its distinctive sound within the wider movement of progressive rock in Europe in the 1970s.

Broader research questions include:

  • How can we identify concrete characteristics that create culturally, geographically and generationally specific sounds?
  • How might cultural imaginaries shape our reception and perception of sound?
  • How and to what extent do the signature sounds of individual artists define what we perceive as collective sound identities, such as bands, genres and scenes?

The current map is a work-in-progress prototype created using the Claude AI. Data was sourced through cross-referencing album covers with credits listed on Discogs and Wikipedia. Genre categorizations were assigned based on compositional form and playing styles observed through listening, and/or through genre and style categories attributed on Discogs.

User Guide

Navigating

  • Pan — Two-finger scroll to pan vertically and horizontally; click + drag to pan in any direction
  • Zoom — Pinch (trackpad) or Ctrl + scroll; double-click on blank space to zoom in; use −/+/↺ buttons (bottom right)

View

  • 📀 / 👤 button (top right) — toggle all cards between album cover and player view
  • Album view (default) — all cards show album cover art; click a card to flip to player view for that card; click again to flip back
  • Player view — all personnel lists visible; click +N more for long lists; click a card to flip to album cover view; click again to flip back

Tracing connections

  • Click a player pill to highlight that player's connections across all albums; click up to 4 players at once to see their interactions
  • Click a group name to highlight all albums by that project
  • Click a player in the legend to open their full profile in the side panel
  • Clear button to reset all selections

Filtering

  • Click a year header to filter by that year; click again to reset
  • Search box — type a player or group name to highlight all their appearances across the map
  • Type @instrument (e.g. @flute) to find all players with that role and list them in the side panel
  • Type @instrument genre (e.g. @bass prog) to filter by instrument within a specific genre
